Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Overview

Amin AI ("the App") is a personal AI assistant built and operated by Mohamedamin. This privacy policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

By using Amin AI, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Access

When you sign in with Google, we may access the following:

  • Your Google account name and email address — used only to identify your session
  • Basic profile information (profile picture) — displayed within the app interface
  • Google Drive files — only if you explicitly grant Drive access, used to read or reference files you choose

How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize your experience within the App
  • To process your requests through the AI assistant
  • To maintain your session while you use the App

We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal information to any third party.

Data Storage

Amin AI stores minimal data locally in your browser (localStorage) including:

  • Your session token to keep you signed in
  • Generated image history for your convenience
  • App preferences and settings

No personal data is stored on external servers controlled by this App.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Groq API — processes your text prompts via Llama AI models
  • Google OAuth — handles secure sign-in
  • Cloudflare Workers — proxies image generation requests

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Your Rights

  • You can revoke Google access at any time via myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • You can clear all local App data by clearing your browser's localStorage
  • You may request deletion of any data by contacting us
